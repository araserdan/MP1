Welcome to Egg Pie ni Diday!

With a passion for crafting delectable egg pies,

Our Journey

Our story began with humble beginnings, rooted in the heart of family tradition. The original egg pie recipe, crafted by my mom, ignited our culinary journey. From the confines of my small kitchen, I embarked on a mission to bring this cherished recipe to life.

Our Pies

Each egg pie we create is a testament to our dedication. Made-to-order, every pie is a promise of freshness, straight from the oven to your plate. Our evolution has been guided by the essence of tradition and an unwavering commitment to quality.

Our Passion and Mission

At the core of our journey lies a profound love for baking. Today, we share our passion with you through each delectable slice of egg pie, delivering an authentic taste that resonates with the flavors of the past.
